Resorts

Kawawii Island has an incredibly diverse series of ecosystems, each packed full of activities and things to enjoy! Our resorts include the following:

Marine Resort

Craving a tropical island getaway? Our marine resort has you covered! With activities like jet skiing, scuba diving, and skydiving, you won't be bored for a moment! Afterwards, head to our welcome center and lodge to sip on some margaritas while watching your friends get smoked in a jet ski race by an anime girl! Truly the beach experience of a lifetime.

City Resort

Crank some 360s and grinds on a skateboard or play some rigged carnival games at the city fair! Feeling particularly brave? Get behind the wheel of a sports car in our racing stadium, no license required! Roaming the city streets has never been this much fun!

Snow Resort

For those of you that like the cold for some reason, our snow resort has incredible views, adrenaline pumping activities, and a relaxing lodge to rest in after you break your bones falling from Tonga Blanca! Surgeons are on standby to make your vacation as pain-free as possible! (morphine at extra charge)

Mountain Resort

Our mountain resort has all the redneck American and snooty British activities your heart desires! From skeet shooting and quickdraw tests with minimal training to horseback riding and tennis for the boring visitors, the mounatin resort has it all! Just watch out for the waterfalls when kayaking.
